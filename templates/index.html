Guardians of the Garden

This website allows users to upload images of tomato or potato leaves and analyzes them for diseases. Our advanced algorithms quickly identify common leaf diseases, providing users with valuable insights to protect their plants and ensure a healthy garden. Join us in safeguarding your garden today!

Our Mission

Our mission is to eliminate waste crops by identifying crop diseases even before they spread to a non-curable portion. We aim to empower farmers with the knowledge and tools needed to protect their yields and contribute to global food security.

Our Services

Our services include offering a platform where farmers can easily upload images of their crops for disease analysis. Our website leverages advanced algorithms to quickly diagnose common and rare plant diseases, providing timely insights and actionable advice to farmers worldwide.
